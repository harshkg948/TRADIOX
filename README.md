View your app in AI Studio: https://ai.studio/apps/4cd439db-9c4a-46e0-b549-93572d338a1b

## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`
# 🌿 TRADIOX — AI-Powered Trading Companion

TRADIOX is an AI-powered trading companion designed to bring calm, clarity, and organic growth to the often chaotic world of finance.

Instead of overwhelming dashboards and complex charts, TRADIOX introduces a **nature-inspired interface** combined with **advanced AI intelligence** to help users make smarter financial decisions.

---

## 🧠 AI-Driven Intelligence

- **Market Analysis**  
  Uses AI (Gemini API) to analyze stock trends, risk factors, and future outlook.

- **Nature AI Assistant**  
  Voice-enabled assistant that understands natural language queries like:  
  *“Invest ₹5000 safely”* or *“Analyze Tesla stock”*

- **Trading Academy**  
  AI-curated learning system providing personalized lessons from basics to advanced strategies.

---

## 📊 Portfolio & Market Tools

- **Real-Time Tracking**  
  Live stock data via secure backend (Finnhub API)

- **Visual Analytics**  
  - 📈 Area Charts for price history  
  - 🍩 Donut Charts for portfolio allocation  

- **Company Profiles**  
  Instant access to company details, logos, and official websites

- **Smart News Feed**  
  AI evaluates market impact of real-time news

---

## 🌿 Nature-Inspired Experience

TRADIOX breaks away from traditional rigid dashboards:

- 🌊 Organic fluid UI (blob shapes & smooth layouts)
- 🍃 Calm, living background animations
- 🪟 Glassmorphism with earthy tones (Sage, Moss, Cream)

---

## 🚀 Vision

To create a trading experience that feels less like stress and more like **growing a tree — steady, intelligent, and intentional.**
